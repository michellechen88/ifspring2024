Fuzhou Peanut Butter Noodles

A simple yet delicious dish! Simply mix the sauce together, toss in your cooked noodles, garnish, and you've got yourself some Fujianese Style Peanut Butter Noodles!

Serves: 3-4

Prepping Time: 5-10 minutes

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

*Optional garnishes: shredded cucumber, chili oil, Sriracha, kimchi, etc.

Allergens

Peanuts, Soy, Wheat, Eggs

Instructions

  1. Mix peanut butter, soysauce and sesame oil in a bowl. Taste the sauce until you find a good balance of flavors. Set the sauce bowl aside.
  2. Boil a pot of water and add the noodles in.
  3. Cook for 1 minute (or 2 minutes if frozen) just until noodles are warm and pliant
  4. Save a cup of the noodle water!
  5. When the noodles are done, scoop them up and place them into the sauce bowl.
  6. Add as little or as much pasta water as you want to get the sauce to a consistency you like!
  7. Garnish and enjoy! Put any leftovers in a tubberware container and keep it in the refrigerator.

Allergens to be aware of: Peanuts, Soy
